Online FSK Modem

Encode text messages into FSK audio signals directly in your browser. Perfect for testing between two computers or browser-to-device communication.

How to Test Between Two Browsers/Computers

1. Setup: Open this page on two different computers with speakers/microphones

2. Configure: Use the same FSK settings on both computers

3. Transmit: Type a message on one computer and click "Encode & Play Audio"

4. Receive: The other computer should be able to hear and decode the message

For best results, use ultrasonic frequencies (22kHz+) to avoid audible interference

Setup Instructions:

  1. Ensure your computer's speakers are turned up

  3. Run the fsk-modem command below on the receiving computer

Configuration Examples:

  • MSX Compatible: -freq "1200,1200" -order 1 -baud 300
  • Ultrasonic: -freq "22000,500" -order 2 -baud 100
  • High Speed: -freq "2000,400" -order 2 -baud 200
