FSK Modem WebAssembly Demo

What is this?
This demo allows you to encode text messages into FSK (Frequency Shift Keying) audio signals directly in your web browser. The generated audio can be decoded by the fsk-modem command-line tool running on a nearby computer.

How to Receive with fsk-modem

Setup Instructions:
1. Ensure your computer's speakers are turned up
2. Position another computer with a microphone nearby
3. Run the fsk-modem command below on the receiving computer
For different configurations:
• MSX Compatible: -freq "1200,1200" -order 1 -baud 300
• Ultrasonic: -freq "22000,500" -order 2 -baud 100
• High Speed: -freq "2000,400" -order 2 -baud 200
